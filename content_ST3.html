How Communities are Helping

Local Beach Cleanups

Coastal communities organize regular cleanups to prevent plastic waste from entering the ocean and harming marine wildlife.

Key Benefits:

  • Immediate removal of plastic.
  • Prevents microplastics.
  • Raises community awareness.

Citizen Science

Everyday people are helping scientists by tracking marine species, reporting coral bleaching, and monitoring water quality.

How they help:

  • Gathering massive data.
  • Tracking endangered species.
  • Spotting pollution early.

Sustainable Seafood

By choosing local, responsibly caught seafood, communities help reduce the pressure of overfishing on vulnerable ecosystems.

What it supports:

  • Protects fish populations.
  • Supports local fishermen.
  • Reduces harmful bycatch.
