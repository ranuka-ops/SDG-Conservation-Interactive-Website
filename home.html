Why Life Below Water Matters

The Ocean's Crisis

Our oceans cover 70% of the planet, but they are under immense threat from plastic pollution and climate changes . Protecting marine ecosystems is vital for global survival.

If we act now:

  • Marine biodiversity will recover.
  • Coastal communities will thrive.
  • Global temperatures will stabilize.

Student & Community Action

As university students, we have the power to drive change. From reducing single-use plastics on campus to participating in local cleanups, every action counts.

3 Things You Can Do:

  • Use reusable water bottles.
  • Recycle campus waste properly.
  • Join a local beach cleanup.

Our Website's Mission

This platform is designed to educate, inspire, and track our collective impact on SDG 14. We aim to turn awareness into measurable, real-world action.

Platform Goals:

  • Spread awareness about marine life.
  • Provide an interactive action simulator.
  • Connect students with volunteer programs.
