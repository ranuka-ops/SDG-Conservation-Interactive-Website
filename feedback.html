Ocean Plastic Clean-Up

Join weekend beach and underwater clean-up drives with local environmental groups. Every piece of plastic removed directly protects marine wildlife from ingestion and entanglement.

Coral Reef Restoration

Volunteer with marine biologists to plant coral fragments and monitor reef recovery at threatened sites around the UK coast and overseas conservation zones.

Become a student ambassador and run awareness workshops teaching children about sustainable fishing, ocean pollution, and what SDG 14 means for their future.
